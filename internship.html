DR. JOHN JOSE

Dr. John Jose works in the domain of multicore computer architecture. His research group in Multicore ARchitecture and Systems MARS Lab Video explore various cutting edge research problems in the following domain. An overview of MARS lab, its members and research works are briefed here. For more details about ongoing research click here.

  • Network on Chip (NoC) and Cache Optimisation in Tiled Chip Multi-Processors (TCMP).
  • Wireless On Chip Interconnects and Edge/Fog Computing.
  • Machine Learning based Domain Specific Accelerators for NoCs.
  • Non-Volatile Memory (NVM) Technology.
  • Secure System on Chip Design Techniques.
  • Disaggregated Memory Management in Data Center Architectures.
